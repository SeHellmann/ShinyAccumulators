Shiny Accumulators

This shiny app visualizes simulations for dynamic confidence models. Based on the DDM (or more general evidence accumulation framework). It simulates paths for the 2DSD, dynWEV and dynaViTE models (see https://doi.org/10.1037/rev0000411).
